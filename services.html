DNS Filtering & Parental Controls

Control internet access and block unwanted content.

See methods & options

There's multiple approaches to content moderation depending on how much control and flexibility you want:

Network-Level Filtering:
Configure your router to block domains across your entire home network. Good for full coverage and simplicity. Your wifi will stop letting devices visit youtube or whatever domain you want.

Device-Level Filtering (NextDNS):
If you don't want certain people accessing certain apps and websites, use services like NextDNS to change DNS settings on individual devices, like smart tvs, and apply urls you don't want them visiting to a denylist. This allows filtering without affecting everyone on the network.
