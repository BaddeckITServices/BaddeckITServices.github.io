Why I Do This

I think I hear the same complaints with technology over again. Often it's about a service. A streaming service doesn't have all the seasons of a show or movies are removed.

Social media or an app or game is unregulated, addictive, could be exploiting it's user base somehow.

Big tech Corporations engaging in anti consumer, and anti competitive behaviors, lobbying to put in laws which give them monopolies over digital software, how you can make payments, and engage with people online.

Corporations like google, apple, microsoft, sony, meta, have financial incentive to keep you hooked on their ecosystem. They want you using google products, and for using google products to be the norm, for it to be weird to use another video hosting platform when you have youtube (where google controls what you're recommended)

Think about it. It's considered weird to use something that isn't the norm. windows & mac, apple & android are normalized because big tech frowns upon open source, privacy focused software line lineageOs and sailfishOS

Many people, use software as a service without realizing it. They think it's fine until the service declines in quality. This is called, platform decay, it is a process in which online products and services decline in quality over time. Initially, vendors create high-quality offerings, attracting users, then they degrade those offerings to better serve business customers, and finally degrade their services to both users and business customers to "maximize short-term profits". As this continues to happen, I wonder why people keep using these services.
